About Lochquarry

Our Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre provides safe, inclusive outdoor adventures for young people aged 6–18. We welcome youth groups and schools, offering unforgettable experiences in Scotland’s stunning landscapes.

Meet Our Team

Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided. Let’s meet some of the Lochquarry staff:

  • Claire Jack
    Centre Manager, responsible for the overall running of the centre and all of its activities. Favourite activity: Pole climb.

  • Robbie Elliot
    Senior Instructor (Land),     overseeing all of the land-based activities. Favourite activity: Hillwalking in the beautiful Scottish highlands.
  • Marion Hunter
    Centre Administrator     responsible for making bookings and arranging activity slots for groups. Favourite activity: Making sure everyone has a great time when they visit Lochquarry.

Our qualified instructors are passionate about outdoor education and ensuring every visitor has a fun, safe experience.
